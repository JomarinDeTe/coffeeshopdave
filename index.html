Our History

Our Story: In 2023, Dave Lander Solomon established The Daily Grind with a vision to offer the finest coffee experience to our beloved community. Inspired by a love for coffee culture and a desire to bring people together over a cup of perfection, our coffee shop has since become a cherished destination for coffee enthusiasts and casual visitors alike.

Our Mission: At The Daily Grind, we are more than just a coffee shop, we are a community. Our mission is to serve each cup with dedication, ensuring every sip brings joy and satisfaction to your taste buds. We take pride in sourcing the finest beans, ethically and sustainably, and perfecting our roasting techniques to bring out the exquisite flavors in every cup.

Quality Over Quantity: Here at The Daily Grind, we are not just about volume; we are about the artistry of coffee. From handcrafted espresso beverages to unique signature drinks, each creation is thoughtfully prepared to tantalize your senses and ignite your coffee passion.

A Home Away From Home: Step into The Daily Grind, and you'll be greeted with the soothing aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the welcoming smiles of our dedicated baristas. Our cozy ambiance and relaxed atmosphere invite you to unwind, catch up with friends, or simply savor a quiet moment alone.

Community and Giving Back: We believe in the power of community, and that's why we are committed to giving back. The Daily Grind proudly supports local initiatives, from partnering with nearby farmers to supporting charitable causes that make a positive impact on our neighborhood.
